One app, two portals

The field and the office, finally in sync

Anything a worker submits appears on the supervisor side under the right project. The field-to-office hand-off is built in — no re-keying, no lost forms.

The crew carries it

Worker Portal

Large, tappable tiles get a worker to what they need in one touch.

  • Dashboard & quick actions — name, project, crew, training status, weather, and a live "Today on site" timeline. One tap to Call Supervisor, Message Crew, dial 911, or route to the nearest hospital.
  • SDS Library — searchable Safety Data Sheets with GHS pictograms, hazard statements, CAS composition, first aid, PPE, flash point, HMIS/NFPA, and Prop 65 flags.
  • OSHA Board — the required postings with plain-language summaries, acknowledgment, and the zoomable official poster.
  • Forms & permits — Hot Work, Confined Space Entry, Heavy Equipment Checklist, and more. Fill, sign on screen, generate a PDF, routed to the supervisor automatically.
  • Toolbox talks — deliver a daily briefing and sign the whole crew in on one device.
  • Portable profile & résumé — documents and certs with expiry alerts, plus a work-experience builder that travels with the worker across contractors.
Superintendents & safety managers

Supervisor Portal

Five areas of oversight, real drill-downs, PDF export throughout.

  • Projects — a portfolio dashboard of active projects, workers on site, open items, and compliance %, with per-project detail and crew rosters.
  • Sign-in sheets — every muster, toolbox talk, and JHA review with signed/total status, a "remind missing" action, and PDF export.
  • Attendance — live IN/OUT toggles per site, on-site counts, and a dropdown to reassign a worker to another job site.
  • Toolbox talks — delivery records by project showing who attended vs. missed, and assign talks from the library.
  • Inspections — a color-coded log (Pass / Open Items / Stop Work) for scaffold, fall protection, crane, and more, with corrective-action tracking and inspector sign-off.
  • Forms hub + JSA/JHA generator — build a Job Safety Analysis from an editable task library and export the full Task → Hazards → Controls → Risk breakdown.

Every paid plan includes both portals — unlimited workers, flat by job site.
