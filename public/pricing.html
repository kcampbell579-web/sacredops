Good to know

What counts as an active job site?

An active job site is a project currently being managed through SacredOps. Completed projects can be archived so you keep access to their documentation without keeping them active in your current plan.

  • Add workers without increasing your subscription.
  • Archive completed projects and retain the records.
  • Upgrade when your number of simultaneous projects increases.
  • Contact us if your project volume changes seasonally.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Do you charge per worker?
No. Every plan includes unlimited workers. Your subscription is based on the number of active job sites.
Do workers need to download an app?
No download required — workers open their portal right in their phone's browser using your company code. Add it to the home screen and it works like an app.
Can I change plans later?
Yes. You can move between plans as your number of active projects and operational needs change.
Is there a setup fee?
No. There are no setup fees on any plan.
Am I locked into a contract?
No. Plans are billed monthly and can be canceled at any time.
What happens to completed project records?
Completed projects can be archived so you retain access to their documentation without keeping them active against your plan's job-site count.
Can subcontractors use SacredOps?
Yes — workers and authorized subcontractors can be added with no per-person charge. Enterprise adds expanded subcontractor-management capabilities.
Can I see the platform before purchasing?
Yes. Use the live demo to tap through the real Supervisor and Worker portals — no signup required.
